The FAQs

Where is Hallabong Jeju?

Hallabong Jeju will take place on Jeju Island, where we will split our program between two parts of the island: Seogwipo in the south and Jeju City in the north.

Who is Hallabong Jeju for?

Hallabong Jeju is for Web3 leaders and builders that want to experience working and exploring one of Asia‚Äôs most picturesque landscapes, while being surrounded by like-minded innovators that can help you with your project or journey.

How do I participate?

Participants will be chosen through an application process that will be announced soon. Once a participant has been approved they can then purchase a ticket for the program.

What is the cost?

The regular program price is $1099, with discounts for approved participants that purchase their tickets early.

What is included?

Your own private studio for five nights, split between Seogwipo and Jeju City as we switch locations. Free access to our designated co-working spaces. Free ticket to our capstone summit event on the last day of the program. Welcome and farewell dinners on the first and last day (other meals are not included).

What if my plans change?

Purchased tickets are non-refundable. However they can be transferred to another person. That other person will have to go through an application process to ensure fit for the program.

Who is organizing Hallabong Jeju?

Hallabong Jeju is being organized by a core group of entrepreneurs with experience hosting social events and workcation programs in the web3 and digital nomad industries.
